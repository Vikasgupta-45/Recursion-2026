Project Growth Engine - Dashboard & Pages Blueprint
===================================================

Based on the capabilities of the "Project Growth Engine" API backend, the frontend application is structured into the following core pages. These pages visually represent the analytics, intelligence insights, predictions, and automated strategies generated by the backend's Python modules.

1. LANDING PAGE
---------------
The entry point of the application before the user enters the dashboard. This page is designed to capture the user's attention and seamlessly transition them into the analytics platform.

Visuals & Sections:
- Hero Section: A bold, modern headline (e.g., "Unlock Your Channel's Potential") with a subtitle explaining the AI-driven growth engine.
- Input Area: A prominent input field where the user can paste their YouTube Channel URL (`youtube_url`) alongside an "Analyze My Channel" call-to-action (CTA) button.
- Loading State: An engaging, animated loading sequence with step-by-step progress indicators (e.g., "Validating Channel...", "Scraping Data...", "Running AI Models...", "Formulating Strategy...") while the backend API (`/api/analyze`) processes the request.


2. MAIN DASHBOARD (OVERVIEW PAGE)
---------------------------------
The Main Dashboard serves as the central hub, giving the user a unified snapshot of their YouTube channel's overall health and recent performance.

Visuals & Sections:
- Top KPI Cards: Clean, modern cards displaying 'Total Subscribers', 'Total Views', 'Total Video Count', and 'Average Engagement Rate'.
- Channel Growth Snapshot: Displaying key data points from "growth_features", such as view momentum over the last 30 days and early success indicators.
- Niche Trend Widget: A mini-chart or indicator displaying the current market trends fetched from Google Trends (e.g., trend score and keyword relevance for your niche).
- Recent Uploads Snippet: A summarized horizontal list or grid of the latest scraped videos showing their views, durations, and publication dates.


2. CHANNEL INTELLIGENCE & PERFORMANCE PAGE
------------------------------------------
This page dives into the "Why" behind the numbers, leveraging the `intelligence_insights` module to analyze what moves the needle for the channel.

Visuals & Sections:
- Performance Drivers Analysis: Visual breakdown highlighting the channel's absolute best posting days, ideal video durations (e.g. Shorts vs Long-form), and top-performing formats.
- Content Insights Panel: Showing detailed analysis on how the channel's historic videos correlate with increased viewership.
- Historical Data Chart: Plotting the clean timeseries data (views, likes, comments) mapped over time.


3. OPPORTUNITIES & AI STRATEGY PAGE
-----------------------------------
A forward-looking page where the AI (Agentic Strategy Engine) identifies weaknesses and lays out a high-level plan.

Visuals & Sections:
- Detected Content Gaps: A list or matrix contrasting the channel's recent video titles against broader macro market trends, explicitly highlighting underserved topics or "gaps" in the market.
- Strategic Roadmap: An AI-generated "Weekly Strategy" detailing recommended content focuses, format distribution, and strategic angles necessary to capture the identified gaps.


4. PREDICTIONS & FORECASTING PAGE
---------------------------------
A highly visual page dedicated to data science and machine learning projections over the next 60 periods.

Visuals & Sections:
- Views Prediction Chart: A 60-day interactive line graph forecasting incoming views.
- Subscriber Forecast Chart: A growth trajectory curve estimating when specific subscriber milestones will be hit.
- Engagement Rate Projection: A visual indicating how audience interaction will trend over the coming months.
- Data Source Indicator: A small status tag noting whether the forecast is leveraging verified "youtube_data_api_v3" records, "ytdlp_metadata", or a "synthetic_fallback".


5. CONTENT PLANNER & OPTIMIZATION PAGE
--------------------------------------
The actionable execution hub where the AI strategy turns into a tangible, day-by-day schedule.

Visuals & Sections:
- SEO & Content Optimizer: Suggestions for high-ranking tags, descriptions, and keywords generated based on the channel's niche, optimized for algorithm discovery.
- 30-Day Content Calendar: A visual, interactive calendar grid. It automatically plots the individual video ideas onto the optimal posting days, mapping out exactly what to record and publish for the entire next month.
